24‑Hour Room Service

Enjoy round‑the‑clock room service complemented by a 24‑hour front desk ensuring your stay is seamless.

Free Wi‑Fi

Complimentary high‑speed internet in rooms and public areas keeps you connected during your hill retreat.

Free Private Parking

Secure, onsite parking with valet and off‑site options, at no extra charge for guests.

On‑site Dining & Breakfast

Enjoy fresh buffet breakfasts and delicious multi‑cuisine meals in our cozy dining restaurant and outdoor area.

Conference & Banquet Facilities
