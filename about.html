About Us

Welcome To SS Silver Oaks Resorts

Welcome to SS Silver Oaks Resorts,Restaurants and Spa, your serene hillside retreat nestled in the lush green landscapes of Yercaud. Located in the peaceful Berry Garden area, our 3-star resort offers a perfect blend of comfort, elegance, and natural beauty—making it an ideal getaway for families, couples, and nature lovers alike.

With 11 well-appointed rooms ranging from Standard to Deluxe options, each space is designed to give you a cozy, home-away-from-home experience. Wake up to the chirping of birds, relax on your private balcony, and soak in panoramic views of the Shevaroy Hills.
